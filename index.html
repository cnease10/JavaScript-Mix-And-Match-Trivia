Web Dev Trivia Game

Welcome to the Web Dev Trivia Game!

Are you ready to test your skills in areas such as HTML, CSS, & JavaScript? Then you've come to the right place!
This is a trivia game where you match the correct answers to the given questions by clicking on the corresponding
answer buttons. You can choose single player by hitting the "I want a challenge button" or you have the option for
two player mode by clicking the "I've brought a friend to duel" button.

One Player Mode

In one player mode, there will be three rounds with three questions in each round. It is your job to match the correct
answer to each question by clikcing on the corresponding answer buttons below the questions. When you have
selected one answer for each question, please hit the continue button to move to the next round.
After round three the continue button will take you to see your final score. Feeling good with your score?
Press the "I want another try" button to challenge a friend.

Two Player Mode

In two player mode, player one will answer three questions for round one, and then player two will answer three
questions for round one. Rounds two and three will be the same way. After round three, the champion of Web Dev Trivia
will be announced. Want another hack at it? Press the "I want another try" button to play again.
